A Power BI dashboard project focused on analyzing **customer segments**, **retention patterns**, and **purchase behavior** to uncover actionable business insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was built to explore how customer transaction data can be transformed into meaningful business insights using **Power BI**.

The dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

- Who are the customers?
- Which customer segments generate the most revenue?
- How many customers return after their first purchase?
- How do customers behave across devices, channels, and payment methods?

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

The main goals of this project were to:

- Identify key customer segments
- Measure customer retention and repeat purchase behavior
- Analyze customer spending patterns
- Understand purchase behavior across channels, devices, and payment methods
- Present business insights through an interactive dashboard

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

The dataset used for this project was intentionally **dirty** and required cleaning before analysis.

### Key preparation steps included:

- Standardizing customer and transaction fields
- Handling missing and inconsistent values
- Creating a clean revenue field
- Building customer-level retention metrics
- Creating supporting tables such as:
  - `Date_Table`
  - `Currency_Table`
  - `Customer_Summary`

---

## 🧠 Dashboard Pages

The dashboard was structured into **3 analytical views**:

### 1️⃣ Customer Overview
Focuses on:
- Total customers
- Total orders
- Total revenue
- Customer segment distribution
- Revenue by segment
- Country-level customer spread
- Monthly purchase activity

### 2️⃣ Customer Retention
Focuses on:
- Repeat vs one-time customers
- Retention rate
- Purchase frequency
- Returning customer trends
- Customer recency distribution
- Repeat customers by segment

### 3️⃣ Customer Purchase Behavior
Focuses on:
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Average quantity per order
- Payment preferences
- Device usage
- Acquisition channel performance
- Spending behavior analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

Some major insights from the dashboard include:

- The **Retail segment** generated the highest revenue and repeat customer activity
- The business achieved an **84% retention rate**
- **Electronics** and **Home** categories attracted the highest customer spend
- **Mobile** was the most used purchase device
- **Transfer** and **Card** were the most preferred payment methods
- Some customers appeared **inactive**, highlighting re-engagement opportunities
- Channels such as **Website**, **TikTok**, and **Facebook** showed strong customer acquisition performance

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the dashboard insights, the following recommendations were made:

- Prioritize retention campaigns for high-value customer segments
- Re-engage inactive and dormant customers
- Improve customer satisfaction and post-purchase experience
- Invest more in strong-performing acquisition channels
- Optimize the mobile shopping experience
- Promote top-performing product categories strategically
